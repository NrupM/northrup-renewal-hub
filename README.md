# Client Retention and Renewals Dashboard

## Current Workflow and Pain Points

- manually exported spreadsheet report
- information edited and not communicated with other employees
- no structured way to track client contact activity per policy
- no prioritisation framework for which policies to focus on
- clients fall through the cracks and there is no structured way to track activity per policy as the company grows

## Workflow Update to Support Growth

> A user can see all policies up for renewal in the next 90 days, prioritised by a risk score that combines time until renewal, premium change %, and historical engagement. They can filter and search to find specific policies, and click into a policy detail drawer to see all relevant information and touchpoints in one place. They can log new touchpoints (calls, emails, notes) directly from the dashboard, and easily send a renewal email that is automatically logged as a touchpoint.

- all while being visible to the whole team and structured for easy handoff as the company scales.
- from a single source of truth, the team can coordinate outreach efforts, ensuring no client falls through the cracks as the company grows.
- replacing the manual reporting, with direct actionability and visibility for the whole team.
